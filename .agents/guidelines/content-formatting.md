# Content Formatting Guidelines

These rules apply when writing or editing any campaign markdown content (adventure files, guides, etc.).

## No Tables

**Never use markdown tables.** Tables do not scale well on mobile phones and tablets (e.g. iPads), which are primary reading devices during game sessions.

**Always use lists instead of tables.** Any information that might naturally be presented as a table should be restructured as a bulleted or numbered list.

### Bad (table)

```markdown
| Monster | HP | AC | Attack |
|---------|----|----|--------|
| Goblin  | 7  | 15 | +4     |
| Orc     | 15 | 13 | +5     |
```

### Good (list)

```markdown
- **Goblin** — HP 7, AC 15, Attack +4
- **Orc** — HP 15, AC 13, Attack +5
```
